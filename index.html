Who we are
Tufts Podcast Network is an platform for student-produced audio storytelling. It was started at Tufts University in 2015 by Cooper McKim. At Tufts, the Tufts Podcast Network publishes audio stories and records live storytelling events.

I’m interested. What should I do next?
There are many ways to get in touch with us, check the bottom of the page and choose one that suits you!
You can also drop by weekly our meeting on Sundays at 8pm in Curtis Hall (at the intersection of Boston and College Ave, attached to Brown and Brew). If you’re a veteran podcaster seeking organizational clout, that’s great! If you’ve never produced a podcast in your life and want to come see what it’s all about, that’s great too! EVERYONE is welcome.
